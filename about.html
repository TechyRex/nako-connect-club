About Nako Connect Club

Learn about our history, values, and what makes our community special

Our History

NAKO CONNECT CLUB (NCC) emerged from likeminded working colleagues at the Muritala Muhammad International Airport cargo section of the airport.

It started as an after working sitting, brainstorming and sharing daily working experiences with the customs clearing agents colleagues after working hours which has been going on for over a decade.

It was suggested and unanimous agreed upon by the foundation members, now called NAKO CONNECT CLUB (NCC) to convert the after work sittings arrangement to a social club before the club was duly registered with Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) on the 24th of August 2023.

Before the registration NAKO CONNECT CLUB (NCC) has been established and engaging in various positive social activities/gatherings that has positively impacted the society.

However until now NAKO CONNECT CLUB (NCC) still strongly believe in our slogan "MOVING FORWARD, ACHIEVING TOGETHER" and also in our motto "PROGRESS, PEACE and UNITY" for coexistence of the club.

Our Core Values

Progress

We are committed to the continuous growth and development of our members and community.

Peace

We promote harmony and peaceful coexistence within and outside our club.

Unity

We believe in the power of togetherness and collaborative achievement.

Membership

Nako Connect Club is open to all professions. We welcome individuals who share our values and want to contribute to our community.
