About Nako Connect Club

Nako Connect Club (NCC) emerged from like-minded working colleagues at the Murtala Muhammad International Airport cargo section. Established in 2017 and officially registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) on August 24, 2023.

Our Mission

To collectively brainstorm on positive aspects of helping each other as club members and giving back to the society with preaching of peace, unity within and outside of the club.

Our Vision

Building a dynamic community where connections flourish and members thrive on empowering for sustainable growth.

Our History

It started as an after-work sitting, brainstorming and sharing daily working experiences with the customs clearing agents colleagues after working hours which has been going on for over a decade.

It was suggested and unanimously agreed upon by the foundation members to convert the after work sittings arrangement to a social club.

NCC strongly believes in our slogan "MOVING FORWARD, ACHIEVING TOGETHER" and our motto "PROGRESS, PEACE and UNITY" for coexistence of the club.
